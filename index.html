About Amene Music

Amene Music brings the vibrant spirit of Polynesian culture to modern beats. Founded in 2025, we're dedicated to showcasing artists who blend traditional island sounds with contemporary music styles.

Our mission is to create a bridge between ancestral rhythms and today's music landscape, celebrating the rich heritage of the Pacific Islands while pushing creative boundaries. From reggae-infused pop to traditional Polynesian instrumentation with electronic elements, Amene Music is where culture meets innovation.
