Camryn Rangassamy

Bio:

Growing up in a Polynesian family, music was the early influence, leading Camryn to self-teach instruments and music production from a young age. Her signature sound is a blend of island music and R&B, characterized by smooth vocals and catchy beats. A love for reggae covers also defines Cam's live performances. Influences include a diverse range of artists like Khalid, Tori Kelly, and High Watah. This fusion of inspiration sets the stage for a compelling new sound in island-infused contemporary music.
